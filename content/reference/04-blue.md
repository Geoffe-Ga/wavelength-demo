Express yourself, making change in the exterior world while looking inward, and witness: there are healthy (temperate) and unhealthy (excessive) dosages of emotional energy. 

(Note that "Leprosy" is a play on Say Anything's song about Bipolar Disorder, This is F*cking Ecstasy, and refers to a sense of too-much-ness that leads to falling apart and being exiled.)

| Phase | Medicinal | Toxic |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rising | Ambition | Voraciousness |
| Peaking | Attunement | Leprosy |
| Withdrawal | Discernment | Self-Medication |
| Diminishing | Conviction | Rage |
| Bottoming Out | Surrender | Misery |
| Restoration | Catharsis | Self-Repression |
