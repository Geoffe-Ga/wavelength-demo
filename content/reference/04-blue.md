Express yourself, making change in the exterior world while looking inward, and you'll see there are healthy and unhealthy dosages of expression.

| Phase | Medicinal | Toxic |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rising | Ambition | Voraciousness |
| Peaking | Attunement | Leprosy |
| Withdrawal | Discernment | Self-Medication |
| Diminishing | Conviction | Rage |
| Bottoming Out | Surrender | Misery |
| Restoration | Catharsis | Self-Repression |
