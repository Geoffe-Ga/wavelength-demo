## Heading
The Wavelength's many Modes

## Intro
Like nites in a scale, the Wavelength expresses in **Modes** — different ways of moving through the same six phases. As you scroll through the Modes, each Phase can be expressed through one of two **Dosages**: the [medicinal]{.med} expression (in the Mode's color) and the [toxic]{.tox} overdose (in red).
