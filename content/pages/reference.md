## Heading
The Wavelength's many Modes

## Intro
Every developmental layer is a **Mode** (Inahbit, Express, Collaborate, Integrate, Absorb) with a particular **orientation** (Do/Feel/Both/Neither) — a way of moving through the same six phases. As you scroll through the Modes, each Phase can be expressed through one of two **Dosages**: the [medicinal]{.med} expression (in the Mode's color) and the [toxic]{.tox} overdose (in red).
